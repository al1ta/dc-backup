# dc-backup
dc power backup station for ac low consume devices

I had the problem to avoid flapping powerline outages that caused wifi extenders reboot.
My wifi extenders are cheap AC powered and changing them with DC ones it would have been too much expensive, so I developed this solution for each extender

- 1 DC UPS 
- 1 DC/AC car inverter
- 1 laser cutted shelf from 3mm playwood sheet
- 1 5.5x2.5mm DC Male Plug power supply ends for adapting the car plug for the UPS
